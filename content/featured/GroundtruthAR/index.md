Engineered a first-of-its-kind AR feature mapping all Points of Interest across DLF Cyber City, Gurugram — letting users discover restaurants, cafes, and retail through a real-time Augmented Reality overlay. Presented live at **NASSCOM** as a flagship demo of AR's commercial viability in location-based advertising. During US engagements, collaborated with venue partners to physically place location nodes and engineer blackout regions using x, y, z coordinates within the GroundTruth Location SDK.
