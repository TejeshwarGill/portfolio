One of the earliest engineers on Samsung FLIP — joined when it ran on Windows (C++, wxWidgets) and carried it through the full OS transition to **Tizen** (2016–2018), directly shaping the architecture of what became a globally shipped commercial product. Built the client application enabling USB and Wi-Fi screen and data sharing across multiple simultaneous clients — a core component of Samsung's B2B interactive display ecosystem. Recognized with Project of the Year (2016 & 2017).
