As a **founding engineer**, helped build Samsung MagicIWB (Interactive White Board) Agent from the ground up — the companion app that lets users wirelessly connect phones and laptops to Samsung interactive displays to share screens, files, and annotations in real time. Shaped the early architecture and cross-platform client that became a core part of Samsung's B2B collaboration ecosystem.
