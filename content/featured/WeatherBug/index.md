At GroundTruth, led the migration of the **WeatherBug** app from a fully native codebase to a hybrid architecture — rebuilding the experience in **React Native** across iOS and Android using **TypeScript, Redux, and JavaScript**, while retaining native iOS modules written in **Swift and Objective-C**. The move unified the codebase, sped up feature delivery, and preserved native performance for one of the most-used weather apps on the App Store.
